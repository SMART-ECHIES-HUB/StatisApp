CLOUD ENGINEERING
03
What Cloud Engineers Actually Do
Deploy
Deploy real applications reliably using Azure App Service, AKS, and Functions. Handle infrastructure provisioning and configuration.
Scale
Scale and harden applications with autoscaling, networking, and policies. Design for high availability and fault tolerance.
Secure
Secure with IAM, Managed Identity, Key Vault, and firewalls. Implement least privilege access and encryption.
Monitor
Monitor production systems with Azure Monitor, Log Analytics, and App Insights. Set up alerts and SLOs.
Fix at 2 AM
Fix when it breaks at 2 AM with incident response, runbooks, and troubleshooting. Be the person companies depend on.
Design
Design for reliability, cost optimization, and resilience. Architect solutions that scale with business needs.

Key Insight: The people growing fastest today are not the best coders — they are the ones who understand Infrastructure + Cloud + Systems thinking.
