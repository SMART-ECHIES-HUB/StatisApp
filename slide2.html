THE PROBLEM: Companies are struggling to find engineers who can run applications in production — not just build them. There's a critical gap between building features and operating systems (uptime, security, cost, on-call).

THE OPPORTUNITY: Become the person who can ship, operate, and scale on Azure. The people growing fastest today are not the best coders — they're the ones who understand Infrastructure + Cloud + Systems thinking.

Key Insight: After 15 years in the industry, the most valuable engineers are those who can handle production operations — deploy reliably, scale efficiently, secure properly, monitor effectively, and fix issues when they break at 2 AM.

Your Path: Move from "just a developer" to "someone companies actually depend on" by mastering Azure cloud engineering.
